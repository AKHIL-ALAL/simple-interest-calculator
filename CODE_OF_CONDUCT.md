# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We commit to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for all contributors.

## Expected Behavior
- Use inclusive and respectful language  
- Accept constructive feedback  
- Be professional in interactions  

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or personal attacks  
- Discriminatory or disrespectful comments  
- Intentional disruption of discussions  

## Enforcement
Any violations can be reported to the project maintainer.  
All reports will be reviewed and appropriate action will be taken.
